Why, "The Rock", Paper, Snikt!?

The bad book review podcast "The Worst Best Sellers" has a game they play with their guests where one of the hosts says what Dwayne 'The Rock' Johnson would do if he was a character in the book, the otheer host says what Wolverine would do. The guest then needs to choose between 'The Rock' Wolverine (Snikt!) or paper (Leave the book alone).

Anyway, it was amusing, you should listen to The Worst Bestsellers, they're pretty funny.
